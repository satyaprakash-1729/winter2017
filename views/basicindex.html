Welcome

As a milestone in the journey of Amingaon Police Station comes the launch of this website which facilitates easy public use of many features which will allow us to better serve the people in need and help even those matters which would have otherwise remained unresolved. This website contains the information for contacting any of the junior and senior staff at any time and also allows people to make suggestions and complaints regarding our conduct. Along with these any one can now see any cases registered by them and the progress made in the same, with a feature to request someone's past records.
